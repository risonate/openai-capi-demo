Answer bank

High-probability interview prompts

Why Pixel plus CAPI?

Pixel gives real-time browser context and automatic `oppref`. CAPI gives resilient server-side delivery. Dedup keeps coverage high without inflating conversions.

What is the hardest part?

Not the POST. The hard part is preserving attribution context, stable IDs, consent routing, validation, and discrepancy debugging.

How do you protect user trust?

Collect only what is needed, respect opt-out and consent, avoid unnecessary user fields, and gate spend until measurement is honest.

What do you escalate?

Recurring implementation confusion, missing diagnostics, unclear errors, or API gaps that block repeatable advertiser onboarding.
